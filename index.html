My favourite NES Games
The NES is the first console I ever owned and even though it may not be the most powerful, it will always be my favourite and these are the games I always come back to!
The Top 4
Super Mario Bros. 3 is not quick, it is not noisy, it doesn't even have flashy colours. It's just the greatest game ever made. Full Stop.
Spider-Man: Return of the Sinister Six is not on many people's lists of best games, but I love it and have spent countless hours failing to complete it.
Castlevania, another real classic. I remember it being the most stupidly difficult game to play. I still find it absurdly difficult.
Ninja Gaiden is just fun. The start of a franchise, the start of friendships. Also, a true example of when box art is ahead of graphics.
Up until now, the biggest question in society about video games has been what to do about violent games. But it's almost like society in general considers video games to be something of a nuisance, that they want to toss into the garbage can.
- Shigeru Miyamoto
